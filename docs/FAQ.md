# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## üè† General Questions

### What is TradeHub?
TradeHub is Nigeria's online marketplace where you can buy and sell anything - from electronics to vehicles, fashion to real estate. It's completely free to use!

### Is TradeHub free?
Yes! Creating an account, browsing listings, and posting items for sale is completely free.

### Do I need to download an app?
No, TradeHub works perfectly in your mobile or desktop browser. However, you can add it to your home screen for quick access (it works like an app!).

### What can I buy and sell on TradeHub?
Almost anything legal! Popular categories include:
- Electronics (phones, laptops, tablets)
- Vehicles (cars, motorcycles)
- Fashion (clothing, shoes, accessories)
- Home & Garden (furniture, appliances)
- Real Estate (houses, land)
- And much more!

### What items are not allowed?
You cannot sell:
- Illegal items or services
- Weapons or ammunition
- Drugs or controlled substances
- Counterfeit or pirated goods
- Stolen items
- Adult content
- Anything prohibited by Nigerian law

## üë§ Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?
1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Enter your Nigerian phone number
3. Receive and enter the OTP code sent via SMS
4. Complete your profile with your name and location
5. Start buying and selling!

### Why do I need to verify my phone number?
Phone verification helps keep TradeHub safe by ensuring real people use the platform. It also allows buyers to contact you easily.

### I didn't receive my OTP code. What should I do?
- Wait 1-2 minutes (SMS can be delayed)
- Check you entered the correct phone number
- Check your SMS inbox (not WhatsApp)
- Click "Resend Code" after 60 seconds
- Try again later if your network is having issues
- Contact support if problem persists

### Can I use a phone number from outside Nigeria?
Currently, TradeHub only supports Nigerian phone numbers (+234).

### Can I have multiple accounts?
We recommend using one account per person. Multiple accounts may be flagged as suspicious.

### How do I change my phone number?
Currently, you need to create a new account with your new number. Contact support@tradehub.ng for assistance.

### How do I reset my password?
TradeHub uses phone number verification (OTP) instead of passwords. Simply log in with your phone number and request a new OTP code.

### How do I delete my account?
Contact support at support@tradehub.ng with your request. Please note that deleting your account will remove all your listings.

## üìù Posting Listings

### How do I post a listing?
1. Click "Post Listing" or "Sell" button
2. Fill in all required information (title, description, price, etc.)
3. Upload up to 5 images
4. Review and submit
5. Your listing is now live!

### How much does it cost to post a listing?
It's completely free! There are no fees for posting listings.

### How many listings can I post?
There's no limit! You can post as many items as you want to sell.

### How long does my listing stay active?
Listings stay active indefinitely until you delete them or mark them as sold.

### Can I edit my listing after posting?
Yes! Go to your listing, click "Edit," make your changes, and save.

### How do I delete a listing?
Go to your listing, click "Delete," and confirm. Note: Deleted listings cannot be recovered.

### When should I delete my listing?
Delete your listing when:
- Your item is sold
- You no longer want to sell the item
- You need to create a new listing with different information

### Why isn't my listing showing up?
Possible reasons:
- Your internet connection was interrupted during posting
- You didn't complete all required fields
- There's a temporary technical issue

Try refreshing the page or posting again. Contact support if the problem persists.

### Can I post the same item multiple times?
No, please don't create duplicate listings. It clutters the platform and may result in your listings being removed.

## üì∏ Images & Photos

### How many images can I upload?
You can upload up to 5 images per listing.

### What image formats are supported?
JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP formats are supported.

### What's the maximum image size?
Each image can be up to 5MB in size.

### Why won't my image upload?
Check that:
- File size is under 5MB
- File format is JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- You haven't exceeded 5 images
- Your internet connection is stable

### Can I rearrange my images?
Yes! When editing your listing, you can reorder images. The first image will be the main photo shown in search results.

### Should I use stock photos?
No! Always use photos of your actual item. Stock photos are misleading and reduce buyer trust.

### What makes a good listing photo?
- Clear and well-lit (natural lighting is best)
- Clean background
- Show item from multiple angles
- Include any accessories or packaging
- Show any defects or wear honestly

## üí∞ Pricing & Payments

### How should I price my item?
- Research similar items on TradeHub
- Consider the item's condition and age
- Be realistic and competitive
- Leave room for negotiation if you're flexible

### Can buyers negotiate the price?
Yes! Buyers can contact you to discuss the price. You can accept, decline, or counter-offer.

### Does TradeHub handle payments?
No, TradeHub doesn't process payments. You arrange payment directly with the buyer/seller (cash, bank transfer, etc.).

### What payment methods should I accept?
Common methods in Nigeria:
- Cash (for in-person meetings)
- Bank transfer
- Mobile money (Opay, PalmPay, etc.)
- POS payment

**Always verify payment before handing over items!**

### Is it safe to accept bank transfers?
Yes, but:
- Confirm payment has arrived in your account
- Wait for confirmation SMS from your bank
- Don't hand over item until payment is verified
- Be wary of fake payment screenshots

## üìû Contacting Sellers/Buyers

### How do I contact a seller?
Click "Contact on WhatsApp" or "Call Seller" on the listing page. This will open WhatsApp or your phone dialer.

### Why use WhatsApp instead of phone calls?
WhatsApp is free, allows you to send photos, keep chat history, and is widely used in Nigeria. However, you can also call directly!

### Can I message sellers through TradeHub?
In-app messaging is coming soon! For now, use WhatsApp or phone calls.

### What should I say when contacting a seller?
Be polite and clear:
- "Hi, I'm interested in your [item name]"
- Ask relevant questions about the item
- Discuss price if you want to negotiate
- Arrange a safe meeting place

### The seller isn't responding. What should I do?
- Give them 24 hours to respond
- Try contacting at a different time
- Look for similar items from other sellers
- Some listings may be old or already sold

### Can I report a seller?
Yes! If you encounter suspicious behavior, scams, or inappropriate content, contact support@tradehub.ng immediately.

## ü§ù Meeting & Transactions

### Where should I meet buyers/sellers?
**Always meet in safe, public places:**
- Shopping malls
- Bank branches
- Busy markets
- Police stations (for high-value items)
- Well-lit, populated areas

**Never meet in:**
- Isolated locations
- Private homes (unless you know the person)
- Dark or secluded areas

### Should I bring someone with me?
Yes, it's always safer to bring a friend or family member, especially for high-value items.

### How do I inspect an item before buying?
- Test electronics (bring charger if needed)
- Check for damage or defects
- Verify serial numbers match
- Ask for receipts or proof of purchase
- Take your time - don't rush

### What if the item isn't as described?
- Don't complete the transaction
- Politely explain the issue to the seller
- Walk away if you're not satisfied
- Report the seller if they were deliberately misleading

### Can TradeHub help with disputes?
TradeHub doesn't mediate transactions or disputes. Always inspect items carefully before paying.

## üîí Safety & Security

### Is TradeHub safe?
TradeHub provides the platform, but your safety depends on how you use it. Always follow our safety guidelines!

### How do I avoid scams?
**Red Flags:**
- Prices too good to be true
- Seller refuses to meet in person
- Requests payment before meeting
- Pressures you to act quickly
- Asks for sensitive personal information

**Stay Safe:**
- Meet in public places
- Inspect items before paying
- Trust your instincts
- Don't send money in advance

### What if I'm scammed?
- Report the incident to support@tradehub.ng
- Report to local police if necessary
- Share details to help us prevent future scams

### How does TradeHub protect my information?
- We never share your phone number publicly
- Your personal information is encrypted
- We don't store payment information
- Read our Privacy Policy for details

### Can I trust sellers on TradeHub?
Most sellers are honest, but always:
- Check their profile
- Read listings carefully
- Ask questions
- Meet in public places
- Trust your instincts
