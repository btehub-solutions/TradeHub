## üì± Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit TradeHub**
   - Go to [tradehub.ng](https://tradehub.ng)
   - Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"

2. **Enter Your Phone Number**
   - Use your Nigerian phone number (+234...)
   - Make sure it's a number you can receive SMS on
   - Click "Send Code"

3. **Verify with OTP**
   - Check your SMS for a 6-digit code
   - Enter the code within 10 minutes
   - Click "Verify"

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Enter your full name
   - Select your location (State and LGA)
   - Upload a profile picture (optional but recommended)
   - Click "Save Profile"

**‚úÖ You're now ready to buy and sell!**

## üí∞ How to Sell

### Creating a Listing

1. **Click "Post Listing"** or "Sell" button
2. **Fill in the details:**

   **Title** (Required)
   - Short, descriptive title (10-100 characters)
   - Example: "iPhone 13 Pro 256GB - Excellent Condition"

   **Description** (Required)
   - Detailed description (50-2000 characters)
   - Include condition, features, reason for selling
   - Be honest and accurate

   **Price** (Required)
   - Enter price in Naira (‚Ç¶)
   - Be realistic and competitive
   - Consider negotiation room

   **Category** (Required)
   - Select the most appropriate category
   - This helps buyers find your item

   **Condition** (Required)
   - New: Brand new, never used
   - Used: Previously used, good condition
   - Refurbished: Professionally restored

   **Location** (Required)
   - Select your State
   - Select your LGA (Local Government Area)
   - Helps local buyers find you

   **Contact Phone** (Required)
   - Your WhatsApp number preferred
   - Must be a valid Nigerian number

3. **Upload Images**
   - Click "Upload Images" or drag and drop
   - Add up to 5 images
   - First image is the main photo
   - Use clear, well-lit photos
   - Show item from different angles

4. **Review and Submit**
   - Check all information is correct
   - Click "Post Listing"
   - Wait for confirmation

**‚úÖ Your listing is now live!**

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is TradeHub free to use?**
A: Yes! Creating an account and posting listings is completely free.

**Q: Do I need to download an app?**
A: No, TradeHub works in your mobile browser. You can add it to your home screen for easy access.

**Q: What can I sell on TradeHub?**
A: Almost anything legal! Electronics, vehicles, fashion, furniture, real estate, and more.

**Q: What can't I sell?**
A: Illegal items, weapons, drugs, counterfeit goods, or anything prohibited by Nigerian law.

### Account Questions

**Q: I didn't receive my OTP code. What should I do?**
A: 
- Check your SMS inbox
- Wait a few minutes (can take up to 2 minutes)
- Check you entered the correct phone number
- Click "Resend Code" after 60 seconds
- Try again with a different network if problem persists

**Q: Can I change my phone number?**
A: Currently, you need to create a new account with your new number. Contact support if you need help.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support at support@tradehub.ng

### Listing Questions

**Q: How long does my listing stay active?**
A: Listings stay active indefinitely until you delete them or mark them as sold.

**Q: Can I edit my listing after posting?**
A: Yes! Go to your listing and click "Edit" to make changes.

**Q: Why isn't my listing showing up?**
A: 
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Make sure you completed all required fields
- Contact support if problem persists

**Q: How many images can I upload?**
A: Up to 5 images per listing.

**Q: What image formats are supported?**
A: JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. Maximum 5MB per image.

### Buying & Selling

**Q: How do I pay for items?**
A: TradeHub doesn't handle payments. You arrange payment directly with the seller (cash, bank transfer, etc.).

**Q: Does TradeHub offer delivery?**
A: No, you arrange pickup or delivery directly with the seller.

**Q: What if I have a problem with a buyer/seller?**
A: Try to resolve it directly first. If unsuccessful, contact support at support@tradehub.ng

**Q: Can I negotiate prices?**
A: Yes! Contact the seller via WhatsApp or phone to discuss the price.

**Q: How do I know if a seller is trustworthy?**
A: Check their profile, read their listings carefully, and always meet in public places.
